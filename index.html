Welcome

Hi there! I'm Jonathan Verrecchia, a French software engineer, minimalist, and electronic music producer - amongst other things.

This site is where I document projects I'm passionate about. I would recommend starting with my Minimalism Guidelines and my write up about the Future of Humanity. I produce electronic music as Leaf Adventure, so you can give it a listen if you're into this or curious.

My resume is available here. To sum it up, I used to work at Yelp as a Front-End Engineer in San Francisco, co-wrote a book about HTML5, created Initializr - a popular tool to start HTML5 projects, graduated from EPITA, a French Computer Science school in Paris, and I'm currently working on establishing a lifestyle framework based on minimalism and heavy digitization.

You can find me on Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, and Github.

Thanks for stopping by!
