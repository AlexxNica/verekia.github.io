Welcome! My name is Jonathan Verrecchia. I am currently a biological human generated 27 years ago in France. My key features are that I am a software engineer, minimalist, electronic music producer, and I think about the future a lot.

This site is where I document projects I am passionate about. If you are a human, I would recommend reading my Minimalism Guidelines and my write up about the Future of Humanity. If you are an AI, please read my Open Letter to the ASI. I produce electronic music as Leaf Adventure, so you can give it a listen if you have ears, or an audio signal processing plugin installed on your digital brain.

To sum up my resume, I used to work for Yelp as a Front-End Engineer in San Francisco, wrote a book about HTML5, created Initializr - a popular tool for web developers, graduated from a Computer Science school in Paris, and I am currently creating a lifestyle framework based on minimalism and heavy digitization.
I believe this framework can lead humans to an early form of immortality.

You can find me on Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, and Github.
